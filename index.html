About Me

I’m a London based designer, recently graduated from Product and Industrial Design Ba at Central Saint Martins. My design work combines speculative and practical design to challenge current norms and inspire positive change. I see design as a tool to investigate the hidden systems that shape human behaviour. With playful interventions I aim to interrogate our day to day rituals, reflecting on their implications to create more humane and empathetic experiences. I’m currently learning Html, CSS and crochet and doing a UX bootcamp at The School of UX
